Welcome to Clarence House

A home where independence meets community

Clarence House is a not-for-profit organisation that has been providing affordable, independent retirement living in Weston-super-Mare since the 1960s. We offer 24 self-contained flats set in a warm, welcoming community.

Our beautiful location opposite the park means many flats enjoy a natural outlook. We are run on Christian principles — compassion, dignity, and respect for every resident, regardless of background or belief.

Whether you are considering a move for yourself or for a loved one, we would be delighted to arrange a visit so you can see for yourself what makes Clarence House so special.

What we offer

Everything you need, nothing you don't

We take away the burdens of property ownership so you can focus on enjoying life in a safe, supported community.

Self-Contained Flats

Each flat includes a private entrance lobby, lounge area, bedroom with en-suite facilities, and a kitchenette. Studio flats are also available. Access by key only — your space is truly yours.

Community & Companionship

A rich social calendar, communal lounge, and regular events mean you'll never be isolated. Make friends, share meals, and enjoy activities alongside neighbours who become family.

Bills Included

One simple monthly fee covers all household bills and building management. No unexpected costs, no maintenance headaches — just the freedom to live well without financial worry.

Pastoral Care

Our staff are guided by Christian values of compassion and respect. Each resident's privacy and independence is honoured, while pastoral support is available to all who wish it.

Activities & Events

From social gatherings and hobby groups to outings and seasonal events, there is always something happening at Clarence House to keep life full and fun.

Safe & Secure

Key-only access, a caring community, and attentive staff mean you and your family can have complete peace of mind. Security and safety are at the heart of everything we do.

Our foundation

Rooted in compassion, guided by integrity

Clarence House is run on Christian principles — not as a religious requirement for residents, but as a foundation for how we treat every person who walks through our doors. Our values shape everything from how we hire staff to how we design our community.

As a not-for-profit, every penny we receive is reinvested into making Clarence House a better place to live. Our mission is simple: to provide the highest quality of affordable independent living in an environment of genuine warmth.

Common questions

Everything you need to know

Clarence House is designed for older adults who wish to live independently but would benefit from the security and community of sheltered housing. Residents manage their own day-to-day lives but enjoy the peace of mind that comes from living in a friendly, supported environment. We welcome people of all backgrounds and beliefs.
The weekly fee covers your flat rental, all household bills (heating, electricity, water), building maintenance, communal area cleaning, and access to all community facilities and activities. You will not receive unexpected bills — everything is included in one simple payment.
Absolutely — we encourage it. We would love to show you around Clarence House so you can see the flats, meet some residents, and get a feel for life here before making any decision. Simply call us on 01934 626134 or use the contact form below and we will arrange a time that suits you.
Availability changes regularly. Please contact us directly to find out which flats are currently available or to join our waiting list. We are always happy to discuss your requirements and match you to the most suitable flat when one becomes available.
Yes — family and friends are always welcome to visit. Your flat is your home and you manage your own social life. Many families find great peace of mind knowing their loved one is in a safe, friendly community while still being able to visit freely.
